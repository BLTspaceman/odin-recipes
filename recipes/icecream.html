Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups heavy cream
  2. 1 cup whole milk
  3. 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Pinch of salt

Steps

  • In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar
  • Add the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt, mixing well
  • Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker
  • Churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency
  • Transfer to an airtight container and freeze for at least 4 hours
  • Serve in bowls or cones and enjoy your homemade ice cream!

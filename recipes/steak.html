Steak

Medium Rare

Ingredients:

  1. 12oz lean 93% beef
  2. 1 stick of butter
  3. buffalo seasoning

Steps

  • Buy a steak like a man not a soy boy loser
  • Put the steak on a grill
  • Cook it for 5 minutes on each side
  • Add a stick of butter on top
  • Add buffalo seasoning
  • Let it rest for 5 minutes
  • Enjoy your steak
